Temperature Based Automatic Fan Control

A Temperature Based Automatic Fan Control System is a system that automatically controls the speed of a fan based on the temperature of the surrounding environment. The temperature sensor continuously measures the temperature of the surrounding environment and sends the data to the microcontroller.
